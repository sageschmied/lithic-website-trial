Understanding Lithics

Lithics, or Stone tools, are various objects made of stone that were used by people (and are still being used!) in order to accomplish certain tasks. There are many different types of stone tools according to their prescribed use, no two tools are exactly the same, even though they may have the same function. There are a lot of different elements and factors that are important in understanding these fascinating objects and the people who created and used them. Things like, the type of production, type of tool and type of stone to name just a few. Stone tools have been found around the globe and have continued use in society today. When it comes to archaeology, this one type of material object can tell us a lot about the people who used it, such as the food they ate, the type of environment they lived on and the sort of relationships or trade they did. Lithics are a truly fascinating subject, and I hope you find them as interesting as I do, please feel free to navigate through this website and see all the various pages on lithics.
